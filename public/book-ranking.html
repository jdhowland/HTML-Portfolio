Stormlight Archive by Brandon Sanderson

I have read "The Way of Kings" once and I'm using the audiobook version to refresh the events in my mind before I move on to the second book of the series, "Words of Radiance"

Current books in the series

The Dark Tower by Stephen King

Stephen King's magnum Opus, this series is one of the most fantastic sagas I've had the pleasure of reading. It's a rich western fantasy about a man, a gunslinger whose only drive is to reach the Dark Tower in a world that is "moving on"

Books in the series

  1. The Gunslinger
  2. The Drawing of the Three
  3. The Wastelands
  4. Wizard and Glass
  5. Wolves of the Calla
  6. Song of Susannah
  7. The Dark Tower

Dune by Frank Herbert

Admittedly, I've only read the first book so far. It's rich in philosophy and is closer to a space opera than traditional sci-fi. There are technically several books in the series but only a few were written by Frank Herbet. My intention is to read the first three books in the "Rise of Atreides" saga.

Books in the series I plan on reading

  1. Dune
  2. Dune Messiah
  3. Children of Dune
